Ingredients

  • 10 Wooden Skewers
  • 4 Skinless, Boneless Chicken Thighs (cubed)
  • 4 Scallions, sliced into 1 inch pieces
  • .5 cup Sake
  • .5 cup Soy Sauce
  • 3tbl Mirin
  • 2tbl White Sugar
  • 1tsp Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Soak 10 wooden skewers in cold water for 15 minutes
  2. Thread chicken pieces and scallions onto skewers
  3. Combine sake, soy sauce, mirin, and sugar in small saucepan and bring to boil
  4. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes
  5. Heat grill pan over high heat and brush with vegetable oil
  6. Add skewers and cook until chicken is no longer pink in the middle
  7. Baste frequently with sauce while cooking
