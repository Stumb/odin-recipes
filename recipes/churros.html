Ingredients

  • 2 quarts Vegetable Oil
  • 1 cup Water
  • 0.5 cup Margarine
  • 1 cup All-purpose Flour
  • .25tsp Salt
  • 3 Eggs
  • .25 cup White Sugar
  • .25 tsp Ground Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Heat oil to 360F (180C) in deep skillet. Oil should be 1.5 in deep.
  2. In medium sauce pan, heat water and margarine to boil
  3. Combine flour and salt, stir into boiling mixture
  4. Reduce heat to low and stir vigorously until mixture forms a ball
  5. Remove from heat and beat eggs into mixutre one at a time
  6. Spoon mixture into pastry bag fitted with large star tip
  7. Squeeze out strips of dough directly into hot oil
  8. Fry strips until golden brown, about 2 minutes on each side
  9. Remove from oil and drain on paper towels
  10. Stir together sugar and cinnamon
  11. Roll churros in surgar/cinnamon mixture while hot
