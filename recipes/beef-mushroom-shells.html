Ingredients

  • 8oz Jumbo Pasta Shells
  • 8oz Ground Beef
  • 8oz Minced Button Mushrooms
  • 1 cup Whole Milk Ricotta
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 2 Cloves Minced Garlic
  • 2tsp Dried Oregano
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 15oz Tomato Sauce
  • 1.5 cups Shredded Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F (175C)
  2. Boil large pot of lightly salted water
  3. Stir in shells and return to boil
  4. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender (~10min).
  5. Drain shells and set aside
  6. Heat large skillet over medium-high heat
  7. Cook/stir ground beef until browned
  8. Add mushrooms, cook approximately 5 minutes
  9. Drain and discard fat
  10. Transfer mixture to bowl and cool to room temperature.
  11. Stir ricotta, egg, garlic, and oregano into cooled mixture
  12. Season mixure with salt and Pepper
  13. Add half of tomato sauce to bottom of 9x13 baking dish
  14. Fill each shell with 1 tsp mixture and place filling up in dish
  15. Spread remaining tomato sauce over shells
  16. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese
  17. Bake in oven approximately 25 minutes
